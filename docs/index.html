Back Row

A menu-bar remote control for your Mac, driven by your iPhone.

Download for macOS

Requires macOS 13 or later. Apple Silicon or Intel.

What you get

  • System volume control, with true Core Audio mute
  • Display brightness, Dark Mode, Night Shift, True Tone
  • Tinted display filters at adjustable opacity
  • Sleep, wake, screensaver, and Wake-on-LAN
  • Discovery over Bonjour — no cloud relay, just your Wi-Fi
  • Built-in Check for Updates… so you stay current
